Voidwalker Story

Chapter I — Rise of the Immortal Empire

It is a time of renewed darkness. Long ago, humanity fled a shattered Earth, settling Mars as the last survivors of a planet consumed by war. Thus began the Discovery Era — a 6,000-year age of exploration that unlocked the secrets of the Solar System.

From the ashes of that era rose the Genesis Period, marked by the rise of the Royal Empire. Empowered by the rare element Vigorium found on Neptune, the empire ruled for nearly seven centuries before collapsing under the weight of civil war and corruption.

The Attrition Period followed, a dark age of fractured power. From the chaos, the United Republic emerged — a beacon of democracy that brought peace to the system for 3,497 years.

But peace bred complacency. The Republic's armies were dismantled, its weapons scrapped. Few knew the last royal bloodline had survived. Prince Junius, heir to the forgotten throne, had vanished into the far reaches of the system — waiting, rebuilding, rising.

Now, after three and a half thousand years, they've returned.

In a single, devastating strike, the Immortal Empire destroyed the Republic Senate on Mars, killing its leaders and capturing President Aurelius. The Republic, weak and fractured, must now rally or fall.

The age of peace is over. The Belligerent Period has begun...
